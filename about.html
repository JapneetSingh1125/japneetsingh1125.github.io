Meet Simmerpreet Kaur, APRN, PMHNP-BC

Simmerpreet Kaur is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner based in Allen, Texas. With a background in emergency department nursing and a PMHNP degree from Walden University, she brings both compassion and clinical expertise to her practice.

Simmerpreet specializes in treating individuals facing anxiety, depression, insomnia, ADHD, trauma, and mood disorders. She integrates medication management with holistic approaches, ensuring personalized care for each client.

She provides telehealth consultations for clients throughout Texas, allowing accessible, confidential support from the comfort of home.
